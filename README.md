#KNOT THEORY AND DNA

Welcome to the Knot Theory and DNA project repository! This project explores the fascinating intersection between Knot Theory, a branch of topology, and its applications in the field of biology, specifically in understanding the structure of DNA. Knot Theory studies mathematical knots, which, unlike everyday knots, are closed loops with no ends. This repository contains various resources to help you learn and understand Knot Theory and its biological applications.

#REPOSITORY CONTENTS:
1.Demonstration Video of Knot Theory: A video that visually explains the basics of Knot Theory and demonstrates its concepts through various examples and animations.
2.Presentation on Knot Theory: A comprehensive presentation that covers the fundamental principles of Knot Theory, its mathematical foundations, and its applications, particularly in biology.
3.PDF of Project: A detailed PDF document that provides an in-depth look at the project, including theoretical explanations, diagrams, and references for further reading.
